About Me

Seit Mohaideen

Data Analyst

As a Senior Data Analyst at ZydeSoft, I apply my skills in SQL, Redwood, and Unix to design, develop, and maintain databases and data pipelines for various projects and clients. I also perform data analysis and reporting using various tools and techniques, such as Tableau and Excel.

I graduated from Bannari Amman Institute of Technology with a Bachelor of Engineering degree in Mechatronics, where I learned the fundamentals of mechanical, electrical, and computer engineering, as well as programmi. I have a keen interest in applying my knowledge and skills to solve complex and challenging problems using data and technology. I am motivated by the opportunity to learn new things, collaborate with diverse teams, and deliver value to the business and the customers.
